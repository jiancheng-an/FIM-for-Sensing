Z. Teng, J. An, L. Gan, N. Al-Dhahir and Z. Han, "Flexible Intelligent Metasurface for Enhancing Multi-Target Wireless Sensing," in IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology, vol. 74, no. 12, pp. 19825-19830, Dec. 2025
